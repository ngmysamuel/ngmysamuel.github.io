Smart Fridge Source: Source Private

An IoT solution to the age old question of what's in my fridge.

With TensorFlow and a pre-trained model, we are able to identify common food items that people would place into their fridges. With that information, we can make use of a web dashboard to generate receipes for the user. We can also save on energy costs by reducing the amount of times we open the fridge

Raspberry Pi, Microbit with TensorFlow Lite and SQLite
